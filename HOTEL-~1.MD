# Mobile App Features for Hotel & Resort Management Software
### Competitive Feature Research — Guest-Facing & Staff/Operations Apps

*Prepared for PropSoft · June 18, 2026*

This document maps the mobile-app features offered by leading hospitality/hotel/resort management software vendors, based on their websites, app-store listings, product documentation, and third-party review profiles. Features are grouped into two app types — **Guest-facing** and **Staff / Operations** — with the providers that offer each feature cited inline. A consolidated source list appears at the end.

Providers reviewed: Oracle OPERA Cloud, Mews, Cloudbeds, INTELITY, Canary Technologies, Duve, Stayntouch, Actabl (ALICE), Hotelogix, Agilysys, RoomRaccoon, Maestro PMS, IDS Next, Amadeus Hospitality (HotSOS), Shiji Group, Infor HMS, eZee/Yanolja Cloud, Guestline, SiteMinder (Little Hotelier), Apaleo, Sabre SynXis Property Hub, protel (Planet), WebRezPro, RMS Cloud, plus guest-experience & operations specialists (Revinate, Operto, SuitePad, Quore, Knowcross), resort guest-app specialists (Nonius, Attractions.io, STAY, LoungeUp, HolidayHero), and Bangladeshi vendors (Smart Software, Pridesys, Mediasoft, Glorious IT, HR Soft BD, GCTL, Mysoftheaven, Nizi, Vhromon) — all cited inline below.

**Bangladeshi vendors — note:** Of the local players, only **Smart Software (Smart Hotel ERP)** publishes a verifiable, feature-rich mobile app (live Google Play listing). **Pridesys (PrideHotel)** and **Mediasoft (Pro-Inn)** advertise mobile access but with limited public detail, so their entries below are vendor claims worth confirming via demo. Their common edge is **localization** — Bangla/English UI, Bangladeshi VAT, and local payment gateways (bKash, Nagad, SSLCommerz). Notably, almost none offer a real **guest-facing** app yet — an opening for PropSoft.

## PART A — GUEST-FACING MOBILE APP FEATURES

### 1. Booking & Pre-Arrival
- **Mobile booking engine** — high-converting, mobile-friendly booking to turn site visitors into guests (RoomRaccoon, Mews, Cloudbeds).
- **Pre-arrival registration / digital registration card** — guest pre-fills registration details (often via an email up to ~48h before arrival); PMS auto-updates so front desk and housekeeping can plan ahead (Oracle OPERA Cloud, Mews, Maestro, Guestline GuestStay, protel, WebRezPro).
- **Dynamic check-in forms** — customizable arrival requirements and data collection per property (Mews).
- **Multi-language guest journey** — guest flow and emails available in multiple languages (e.g., nine on OPERA Cloud); Bangla/English bilingual UI from local vendors (Oracle OPERA Cloud; Smart Software, Mediasoft, BD vendors).

### 2. Mobile Check-In / Check-Out
- **Contactless / online check-in** — skip the front desk; complete check-in from a personal device, often with no app download required (Oracle OPERA Cloud, Mews, Canary, Duve, Cloudbeds, Stayntouch, IDS Next, Shiji Digital Stay, Infor HMS, eZee, Guestline GuestStay, Apaleo, protel, WebRezPro, RMS Cloud, Operto).
- **QR-code instant check-in** — guest scans a QR code to check in, upload documents, and add a digital signature (IDS Next FX GeM).
- **Electronic signature capture** — guests sign registration cards / receipts on a phone or tablet; capture signatures and documents from a tablet at check-in (WebRezPro, Smart Software BD).
- **ID scanning & fraud checks** — capture/scan identity documents during check-in (Hotelogix, Canary, IDS Next, eZee).
- **Express mobile check-out** — self-service checkout from the guest's own device (Maestro, Canary, Mews, Shiji, Infor HMS).
- **Self-service kiosk option** — turn a tablet into a digital greeter as a check-in alternative (Mews).

### 3. Digital / Mobile Room Key
- **Mobile key via app** — phone acts as the room key through certified BLE lock integrations (INTELITY, Oracle OPERA Cloud, Mews, Infor HMS, RMS Cloud via OpenKey, Operto / YourKey).
- **Apple Wallet / Apple Watch key** — key lives on the lock screen via Apple + ASSA ABLOY integration (Mews).
- **Lock-vendor partnerships** — integrations with major door-lock providers (Salto, ASSA ABLOY, etc.) (Mews, INTELITY, Oracle).

### 4. In-Stay Services & In-Room Engagement
- **Mobile food & beverage / room-service ordering** — order from any restaurant/bar on property for room or location delivery; orders route directly to staff (INTELITY, Maestro, Shiji, Guestline Keez, Operto, SuitePad, Mediasoft Pro-Inn).
- **In-app maintenance / concierge requests** — guests report issues or request services directly from the app (Guestline Keez, Apaleo).
- **In-room controls** — adjust temperature/lighting and environment from the app (INTELITY, Operto smart-home, SuitePad).
- **Wake-up calls & service scheduling** from the device (INTELITY).
- **Branded guest app for bookings & hotel interaction** — customizable iOS/Android app for reservations, messaging, mobile ordering and upsells (IDS Next FX Club App, Maestro).
- **Digital guidebook / property info & local recommendations** — answers common guest questions without staff involvement; one-touch Wi-Fi, area tips (Oracle OPERA Cloud, Maestro, Operto, SuitePad digital directory, Nonius/STAY/LoungeUp).
- **Green / opt-out housekeeping option** — guests decline daily cleaning for a sustainability perk (SuitePad).
- **Interactive resort map** — tap points of interest (dining, gym, pools, activities) and jump straight to booking; surfaces upsell opportunities (Attractions.io, Nonius).

### 5. Upselling & Ancillary Revenue
- **Room upgrades & add-on packages** — early check-in, late checkout, breakfast, parking, pet fees, F&B (Canary, Duve, Mews, RoomRaccoon, Oracle upgrade-offers page).
- **Personalized / occasion-based upsells** — tailored offers triggered at booking and throughout the stay; reported uplifts up to ~250% (Canary, Duve, Revinate, Operto smart buttons).
- **Experiences & local bookings** — tours, transportation, gift baskets, picnic baskets, three-course meals (Duve, RoomRaccoon).
- **Self-service portal pre-orders** — guests add breakfast, parking, and other ancillaries before arrival (Guestline self-service portal, Apaleo eCommerce).
- **Multi-channel upsell delivery** — offers pushed via SMS, WhatsApp, and email (Canary, Duve).

### 6. Communication & Messaging
- **Two-way guest messaging** — instant chat routed to front desk; works across SMS, WhatsApp, OTA inboxes, email from one hub (Mews, Canary, Duve, Shiji, Apaleo, Guestline Keez, Revinate Ivy, Operto, SuitePad).
- **AI-assisted messaging & auto-translation** — AI suggests replies from past interactions and chats with guests in their own language (Revinate Ivy).
- **Automated room-ready alerts** — segmented, timed messages such as "your room is ready" on the guest's preferred channel (Shiji).
- **AI agents / chatbots** — automated guest engagement across channels (Duve).
- **Push notifications & in-app campaigns** — timed updates, promotions, image + CTA campaigns (Nonius, resort guest-app specialists).

### 7. Payments
- **Pay-by-link / payment links** — guests pay online from a link (Cloudbeds, RoomRaccoon).
- **Integrated & contactless payment** during online check-in/out (Mews, RoomRaccoon).
- **Local mobile payment gateways** — bKash, Nagad, SSLCommerz integration for in-app guest payments (Smart Software, Mediasoft, BD vendors).
- **Digital tipping** — app-less QR-code tipping by individual or department throughout the journey (Canary).

### 8. Loyalty & Personalization
- **Loyalty program / rewards in-app** — redeem points for upgrades, amenities, dining discounts, free nights (resort guest-app pattern; Maestro guest loyalty module).
- **VIP perks & tier tracking** — early check-in, late checkout, priority amenity access; notifications about reward progress (resort guest-app specialists).
- **Guest profiles & preferences** — profile-driven personalization across rooms, dining, spa, golf (Agilysys, Mews Guest Intelligence, Sabre SynXis Property Hub, Revinate CRM).
- **Guest CRM / rich profiles from PMS data** — unified profiles powering targeted campaigns and upsells (Revinate, Cendyn-class CRM).

### 9. Activity & Amenity Booking (Resort-Specific)
- **Activity / excursion planner** — browse categorized activities, book and pay online (Attractions.io, Nonius).
- **Spa, golf, restaurant & activity reservations** — property-wide booking with guest-profile personalization, bookable in-app (Agilysys, Maestro, INTELITY, SuitePad, Operto).

## PART B — STAFF / OPERATIONS MOBILE APP FEATURES

### 1. Front Desk on Mobile
- **Mobile check-in/out, upgrades, charge posting, folios, key encoding** from phone/tablet anywhere on property (Stayntouch, Smart Software BD).
- **Real-time availability & multi-property reservations** from any location via a mobile front-desk app (IDS Next FX Front Desk, Shiji).
- **Single-device PMS + POS + payments** — staff manage check-in, dining, spa, upsells and payments on one mobile device, removing silos (Shiji).
- **Next-arriving-guest view** — see and edit guest preferences, messages, and notes on mobile (Infor HMS).
- **Guest-centric mobile profiles** — staff access full guest profiles/preferences on any device; fast onboarding (Sabre SynXis Property Hub).
- **Mobile / no-download web PMS access** — run front- and back-office operations from any phone or tablet browser (WebRezPro, RMS Cloud staff portal, Mediasoft Pro-Inn, Pridesys).
- **Offline mode with auto-sync** — staff keep working without internet; data syncs when reconnected (Smart Software BD).
- **Arrivals / departures / in-house views** with guest & reservation search (Cloudbeds, Hotelogix, eZee).
- **Rate & rack-rate changes** (seasonal/dynamic) in a few taps (Hotelogix).
- **Process guest payments** on the go (Stayntouch, Hotelogix, SiteMinder Little Hotelier).

### 2. Reservations Management
- **Calendar view of bookings & room blocks** — color-coded, drag-and-drop, extend stays, tap for details (RoomRaccoon, Cloudbeds).
- **Assign rooms & add notes** to reservations/guests (Cloudbeds, eZee).
- **Create/edit reservations & room closures on mobile** with instant booking push notifications (SiteMinder Little Hotelier).
- **Generate Pay-by-Link** and send to guests from the app (Cloudbeds).

### 3. Housekeeping Management
- **Real-time room-status updates** — clean/dirty, vacant/occupied, attendant-in-room, out-of-service; "Clean / Dirty / Inspected" tracking (Stayntouch, Maestro, Hotelogix, IDS Next FX Housekeeping, Amadeus HotSOS, Infor HMS, eZee, Smart Software BD, Pridesys, Mediasoft).
- **Supervisor–housekeeper communication & progress tracking** — managers assign tasks dynamically and track cleaning progress from handhelds (IDS Next FX Housekeeping, Amadeus HotSOS).
- **Workload tracking by credits / minutes / area** — room assignment respecting local labor rules, multi-language (Amadeus HotSOS).
- **Per-room guest details & "Do Not Disturb" flags** — housekeepers see occupancy and defer DND rooms (Infor HMS Housekeeper, protel).
- **Cleaning & inspection checklists / brand-standard QA** — digital checklists, deep-clean tracking, room inspections (WebRezPro, Quore, Knowcross).
- **Minibar consumption & laundry/towel tracking** logged from the room (Quore, protel).
- **Replaces printed attendant worksheets** — fully digital, real-time PMS sync (protel, WebRezPro).
- **Auto room assignment & task lists** — drag-and-drop custom assignments; auto-generate by employee work time (Stayntouch, ALICE/Actabl).
- **Instant front-desk notification** when a room is ready — enables more early check-ins (Stayntouch).
- **Mobile task reassignment** — managers adjust workloads in real time without radios (ALICE/Actabl).
- **Runs on staff or hotel-provided devices** (iOS, Android, Windows handhelds, tablets) (Maestro).

### 4. Maintenance & Work Orders
- **Work-order creation & tracking** with notes and photos across shifts; log, assign and track repairs request-to-completion (ALICE/Actabl, Maestro work-order module, Quore, protel, WebRezPro maintenance alarms, Smart Software BD).
- **Service orders & space inspections on mobile** — generate orders and run guestroom/public-area inspections via role-based access (Amadeus HotSOS).
- **Guest-request & incident management** — log, route, and track guest requests and incidents (Knowcross, Quore).
- **Preventive maintenance & asset history** — track all hotel asset history and equipment status; log PM tasks and readings (pool, etc.) from mobile (ALICE/Actabl, Quore).

### 5. Task Management & Internal Communication
- **Cross-department service requests** — send/receive tasks anywhere on property (ALICE/Actabl).
- **Two-way desktop↔mobile staff messaging** — real-time collaboration between back-office and field staff (Amadeus HotSOS).
- **Concierge & lost-and-found task management** on mobile (Infor HMS).
- **Guest-service assignment & monitoring** — managers/supervisors assign and track guest-request progress from a mobile app (IDS Next FX Guest Service).
- **Unified ops platform** connecting all departments for messaging and accountability (ALICE/Actabl).
- **Operational analytics** on task completion and labor (ALICE/Actabl).

### 6. Notifications & Alerts (Staff)
- **Real-time push notifications** for new bookings, cancellations, guest messages; filterable, with history (Cloudbeds, SiteMinder Little Hotelier, eZee).
- **Multi-property notifications** — alerts across all accessible properties, not just the active one (Cloudbeds).

### 7. Dashboards, Reporting & Analytics
- **Role-based dashboards** — Manager, Front Desk, and Housekeeping views (Stayntouch).
- **KPIs on mobile** — ARR, RevPAR, occupancy, house status, revenue analysis, business-on-books, top OTAs (Hotelogix).
- **Occupancy meter & booking-trend insights** in-app (Cloudbeds).

### 8. Point of Sale & Multi-Outlet (Resort)
- **Manage multiple POS outlets** — room service, restaurants, laundry, travel desk; table/room/takeaway modes (Hotelogix).
- **Mobile POS & tableside ordering** — take orders and process payments at the table; inventory & recipe costing (Smart Software BD).
- **Multi-venue POS** across restaurants, bars, poolside, spa, golf, retail, casino with centralized reporting (Agilysys InfoGenesis).
- **Spa & golf operations** — appointment scheduling, resource coordination, tee-time booking, revenue optimization (Agilysys, Maestro).

### 9. Revenue Management (Mobile-Accessible)
- **Automated dynamic pricing** — rates adjust to demand, occupancy, booking patterns (RoomRaccoon RaccoonRev).
- **Manage rates & groups** from a tablet (Stayntouch).
- **Live market intelligence on mobile** — act on pricing, inventory and distribution from the phone (SiteMinder Little Hotelier Dynamic Revenue Plus).
- **Channel manager sync from mobile** — auto-sync availability/rates across Booking.com, Expedia, and the hotel website (Smart Software BD, eZee).

## Recommended Must-Have Feature Set for PropSoft

**Guest app (MVP):** mobile booking → online/contactless check-in → digital/mobile key → in-app messaging → F&B & service ordering → upsells → mobile checkout & payment. Add interactive map + activity/spa/golf booking + loyalty for the resort segment.

**Staff app (MVP):** mobile front desk (check-in/out, payments) → reservations calendar → real-time housekeeping status & assignment → maintenance/work orders → task management & cross-department messaging → push alerts → mobile dashboards (occupancy, ARR, RevPAR). Add multi-outlet POS for resorts.
